Confidence Interval

信賴區間（Confidence Interval）

The confidence interval 1−α1-\alpha1−α for μ\muμ is

[X‾−tn−1(1−α2)Sn,X‾+tn−1(1−α2)Sn]\left[\overline{X} - t_{n-1}(1-\frac{\alpha}{2})\frac{S}{\sqrt n}, \overline{X} + t_{n-1}(1-\frac{\alpha}{2})\frac{S}{\sqrt n}\right] [X−tn−1​(1−2α​)n​S​,X+tn−1​(1−2α​)n​S​]

The confidence interval 1−α1-\alpha1−α for σ2\sigma^2σ2 is

[(n−1)S2χn−12(α2),(n−1)S2χn−12(1−α2)]\left[\frac{(n-1)S^2}{\chi^2_{n-1}(\frac{\alpha}{2})}, \frac{(n-1)S^2}{\chi^2_{n-1}(1-\frac{\alpha}{2})} \right] [χn−12​(2α​)(n−1)S2​,χn−12​(1−2α​)(n−1)S2​]
